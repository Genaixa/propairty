Court Proceedings

After the expiry of {{ notice_days }} days from the date of service of this notice, the Landlord intends to apply to the County Court for an order for possession. The court will fix a date for the hearing and you will be served with notice of the hearing date.

You should seek independent legal advice as soon as possible. You may be able to prevent possession proceedings if you pay all outstanding rent before the date of the hearing. However, if you are relying on Ground 8, the full arrears must be cleared before the hearing for the court to refuse the order.

Right to Appeal: You have the right to ask the court to postpone the date for possession. The court may do this if it considers it reasonable. You may also apply for legal aid if you qualify. Contact your local Citizen's Advice Bureau for free advice.
