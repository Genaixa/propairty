3. HMO Management Regulations

Even if a full licence is not required, properties occupied by 3 or more persons from 2 or more households are subject to the Management of Houses in Multiple Occupation (England) Regulations 2006. As manager you must:

  • Ensure all means of escape from fire are maintained in good order and kept free from obstruction
  • Ensure fire fighting equipment and fire alarms are maintained
  • Ensure all common parts and fixtures are maintained in good repair
  • Ensure all windows and other means of ventilation are maintained
  • Ensure all gas and electrical supply systems are maintained in safe condition
  • Ensure adequate drainage and water supply
  • Provide each occupier with written information about their manager's name, address, and contact details
