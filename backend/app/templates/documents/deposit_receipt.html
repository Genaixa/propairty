Deposit Protection

In accordance with the Housing Act 2004 (as amended by the Localism Act 2011), this deposit has been or will be protected in a Government-approved Tenancy Deposit Protection scheme within 30 days of receipt.

You will receive (or have received) the Prescribed Information relating to the deposit protection scheme separately. Please retain this document for your records.

Deductions Policy

At the end of the tenancy, your deposit may be used to cover:

  • Unpaid rent or other monies owed under the tenancy agreement;
  • Damage to the property or its contents beyond fair wear and tear;
  • Cleaning costs where the property is not returned in the same condition;
  • Any breach of your obligations under the tenancy agreement.

Any deductions will be agreed with you or determined by the deposit protection scheme's dispute resolution service.

Return of Deposit

Subject to any agreed deductions, the deposit will be returned to you within 10 days of the end of the tenancy and agreement of any deductions.
