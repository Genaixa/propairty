  TENANT OBLIGATIONS

  The Tenant agrees to:
  1. Pay the rent on the 1st of each month.
  2. Keep the Property and its contents clean and in
  good condition throughout the tenancy.
  3. Report any disrepair or damage to the Landlord or
  managing agent promptly.
  4. Not make structural alterations or redecorate
  without prior written consent.
  5. Not sublet the Property or any part of it without
  prior written consent.
  6. Allow the Landlord or their agent access for
  inspections or repair works upon 24 hours' written
  notice, except in emergencies.
  7. Not keep pets at the Property without prior written
   consent.
  8. Not smoke inside the Property.

  LANDLORD OBLIGATIONS

  The Landlord agrees to:
  1. Keep in good repair the structure and exterior of
  the Property.
  2. Maintain all installations for the supply of gas,
  water, electricity, sanitation and heating.
  3. Provide a valid Gas Safety Certificate annually,
  carried out by a Gas Safe registered engineer.
  4. Ensure electrical installations comply with current
   regulations and provide an EICR.
  5. Maintain a valid Energy Performance Certificate
  (EPC) for the Property.
  6. Provide quiet enjoyment of the Property to the
  Tenant throughout the tenancy.

  SPECIAL CONDITIONS

  1. A professional end-of-tenancy clean is required, to

  2. Any damage beyond fair wear and tear will be
